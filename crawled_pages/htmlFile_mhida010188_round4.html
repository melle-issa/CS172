Undergraduate Academic Programs

 

The Office of Undergraduate Academic Programs facilitates the planning and coordination of academic curriculum and programs for undergraduate education and general education in accordance with system-wide and university policies. This office ensures the quality of curriculum and academic programs provide a transformative learning experience for all Titans. This office facilitates and responds to requests for new curriculum and curricular changes.
