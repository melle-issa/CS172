Enroll in Cal State Fullerton courses

Extension and International Programs expands access to University classes through Open University, Summer Session and Winter Session. Open University registration is open to the general public; Summer and Winter Sessions help Cal State Fullerton students graduate on-time or early.

Enrollment in Cal State Fullerton’s undergraduate and graduate classes is open to the public on a space-available basis.

Explore lifelong learning opportunities

Our lifelong learning opportunities range from programs for children to retirees, and include an on-campus arboretum with classes and events for those of all ages.

Titan Youth Programs

Programs for children in STEM, technology, academics and other enriching learning experiences.

Fullerton Arboretum

Our twenty-six acre Arboretum offers classes and activities to Cal State Fullerton students and the general public.

Osher Lifelong Learning Institute

Classes and opportunities for active, older adults to join a learning community housed on a university campus.
