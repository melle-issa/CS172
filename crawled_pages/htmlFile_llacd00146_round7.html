Parents & Families

Our brilliant and vibrant students are supported by engaged parents and families who share Brown's commitment to prepare them for lives of "usefulness and reputation."

Brown prepares its students to forge their own paths.

We invite parents and families to journey alongside them.

Parents and Family Information

Parents and families are an important part of the Brown community. We invite you to get involved and learn about the unique academic community that is Brown.

Medical School Parents and Family Information

As members of the Brown medical community, parents and families have direct access to the Medical School’s parents and alumni relations team who can help answer your questions and connect you to resources.

Parent & Family Giving

As part of our vibrant and proud extended family, you sustain and strengthen Brown's distinctive brand of academic excellence and enhance educational opportunity for all students through your generosity.

Parents Leadership Council

Through their philanthropic support and volunteer outreach, members of the Parents Leadership Council have made The Parents Fund the top parent fundraising program in the Ivy League.

Need more information?

Email families@brown.edu
