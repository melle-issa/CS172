Faculty Opportunities Abroad

Chico State Faculty have various opportunities available to teach abroad:

  • Director of a short-term Faculty-Led Program. 
  • Visiting Professor on the  University Studies Abroad Consortium(opens in new window) (USAC) during the summer, winter intersession, fall, or spring semester. 
  • Resident Director through CSU, International Programs (CSUIP). (opens in new window)
  • The Wang Family Stipend(opens in new window) for research or teaching abroad in China.
  • Fulbright Scholar Program(opens in new window) conducting research abroad.
  • Visiting scholars through one of our currently established MOUs with an institution abroad.  
  • COIL through virtual collaboration and exchange with other faculty and universities around the globe. 

For more information on international opportunities for Chico State Faculty and Staff, please contact the Study Abroad & Exchange Coordinator at 530.898.5415.

You can also watch the following webinar that we hosted in the past regarding faculty-led programming:

  • Open the Door to the World for Students:(opens in new window) Learn about short-term global experiences from WorldStrides, an ally of Chico State. In this recording, WorldStrides shares how to create an educational experience abroad that aligns with faculty learning objectives.
