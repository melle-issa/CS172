For the Media

If you’re interested in talking to any of these experts, contact the Office of Marketing and Communications.

California State University, Northridge’s Office of Marketing and Communications offers assistance to print, broadcast and online media. Our staff can connect reporters with faculty experts, assist news crews with shooting on campus or help members of the media locate archived photos, news stories and media releases.

For additional information or help, contact:

Media Releases: Visit CSUN Today or browse the Media Releases website for the latest releases from the university. To receive CSUN media releases via email or regular mail, email pubinfo@csun.edu or call Media Relations at (818) 677-2130.

Find an Expert: Members of the media can contact Media Relations for assistance in finding experts who will comment on news stories. Reporters can also browse the university’s Experts Directory (PDF). If you are a member of a news organization and would like a hard copy of the director, email pubinfo@csun.edu or call (818) 677-2130
