Do the Woo Joins WordCamp Europe 2023 as Media Partner

April 26, 2023

For the second year, Do the Woo will be joining several others great companies as a Media Partner at this year's WordCamp Europe in Athens.

Last year we enjoyed not only keeping everyone updated on the event, pre-, during and post WordCamp, but also had fun with a few podcasts. Pre-WordCamp, we interviewed two of the organizers and published a collection of builder tips from the speakers. While at the event, we also did several podcasts including a conversation with Augustin from Weglot, Silvana Pop as a first timer at WordCamp, a Woo BizChat with Robert Windisch and Vito Peleg, some talk on SEO with Maireke van de Rakt, and more talks with Milan Ivanovic, Daugirdas Junkus, Boggie Yanishen and David Mainayar.

As you can see I and several of my co-hosts were busy during the event. In hindsight I have learned podcasting is tough at live events. The most challenging part is carving into someone else's schedule. Also, I ended up missed a lot of opportunities to meet new people and catch up with old friends.

This year at WordCamp Europe

Of course as a media partner, we will be covering the event in many ways. And you can expect some podcasts as well. Whenever I attend a flagship WordCamp, I will spread the good news, media partner or not.

But more importantly I want to meet as many of you as possible. Instead of scheduling times, I will be spending most of my time in the sponsor area. I may even tweet out if I'm hanging at one of our own Do the Woo sponsor booths. So please make sure and say hi and always happy to have a bit of a chat.

And if you haven't gotten your ticket for this amazing event in Athens Greece, you can do that here.

Hope to see you there!

Thanks to our Pod Friends for their support
