Brown Annual Fund

As one of the University's most powerful giving vehicles, the Brown Annual Fund provides the immediate-use support that fuels the Brown experience.

Making Brown accessible to the most talented students from all backgrounds. Keeping the University innovative and competitive. Ensuring our commitment to academic excellence and service to society. 

The Brown Annual Fund does all that, and much more. With its immediate-use funds, it empowers the University to nimbly respond to today's most pressing needs and emerging opportunities.

Where to Give

The Annual Fund is a "family of funds" that encompasses Brown's annual giving opportunities. Together, these funds touch nearly every nook and cranny of life at Brown.

The Brown Fund

Supporting the University where it's most needed, from academics to campus-wide programs to financial aid.
Visit Page

Athletics Annual Fund

Making an immediate impact for Brown’s student-athletes.
Visit Page

Impact Funds

Each impact fund will strengthen a specific, high-priority initiative of the University. For Brown Annual Fund supporters, it offers targeted philanthropic opportunities to support presidential initiatives that align with their interests.

Pathways to Diversity and Inclusion Fund

Creating an inclusive, diverse, and vibrant academic community at Brown.
Visit Page

BrownConnect Fund

Ensuring all students have access to summer internships and research experiences.
Visit Page

IPC Brown Annual Fund Scholarship

Providing financial aid for exceptional African American and Black students at Brown.
Visit Page

Campus Sustainability Fund

Reducing our carbon footprint and making Brown a net-zero energy campus.
Visit Page

Professional School Funds

These school-specific funds directly support the individual priorities of each professional school. The Brown Medical Annual Fund, the School of Public Health Annual Fund, and the School of Engineering Annual Fund are the bedrock of our professional schools' immediate-use initiatives.

Brown Medical Annual Fund

Helping to educate the next generation of physician leaders at The Warren Alpert Medical School.
Visit Page

School of Engineering Annual Fund

Continuing the School of Engineering's reputation as a leader in education and driving advancements in research and learning.
Visit Page

Public Health Annual Fund

Advancing the School of Public Health's high impact work in improving human health for all.
Visit Page
