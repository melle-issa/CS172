Working at UC-Illuminating what's possible

Working at UC means being part of this vibrant institution that shines a light on what is possible.

You may teach students or feed them, develop the latest flu shot or give it, write books or shelve them, invent new crops or plant them; whatever your role, you play an important part in making things better for individuals, for California, for the world.

People make UC great, and UC recognizes your contributions by making this a great place to work. Excellent retirement and health benefits are just one of the rewards.
