The 2022–2023 Season

The Wizard of Oz: A Journey to Find That There is No place Like Home

As spring rain clouds drift to make way for the bright rays of summer, feelings of nostalgia can be found somewhere over the rainbow in the Chico State Music and Theatre Department's production of "The Wizard of Oz"

This evocative show recounts the tale of farm girl Dorothy Gale as she travels over the rainbow from Kansas to the mystical Land of Oz. There, she meets an ensemble of characters including the Scarecrow, Tin Man and the Lion as she discovers that there truly is no place like home.

The spring musical opens at Laxson Auditorium on Thursday, May 4, with 7:30 p.m. performances through May 6. There will be a 2 p.m. matinee on Saturday, May 6, and the final performance is at 2 p.m. on Sunday, May 7.

The Chico Chapter of the Chico State Alumni Association is hosting a reception following the Sunday matinee. Alumni who wish to attend Alumni Day at Laxson mustregister online at https://tinyurl.com/oz-reception(opens in new window). For more information contact the Alumni Association at 530-898-6472.
