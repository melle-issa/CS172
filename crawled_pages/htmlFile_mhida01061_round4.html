Recognizing Our Donors

If CSUF students and faculty are the heart of our University, then donors are the backbone.

The ways to make a difference are as diverse as our donors: scholarships; endowed chairs and professorships; special programs and centers; the building and renovation of facilities. We salute the many donors since our founding who have helped CSUF become the exceptional institution it is today, and invite you to join their distinguished ranks.


2020 Honor Roll   Ontiveros Legacy Society

  • Lifting Up Former Foster Youth

    "This amazing Guardian Scholars program has changed, and quite possibly, saved my life,” says Jorge Cardenas.

  • Car Collection Fuels Scientific Research

    The groundbreaking detection of gravitational waves prompted Nicholas and Lee Begovich, to make one of the largest philanthropic investments in the history of Cal State Fullerton — a collection of 14 classic sports cars valued at $10 million.

  • Lifeline for Families in Need

    CSUF’s Center for Healthy Neighborhoods serves families living on an annual income of less than $25,000. The donor-supported center provides mental health services, career planning and development, leadership training, health and fitness classes, parenting workshops, academic and art programs for youth.

  • Great Returns on a Titan Investment

    With visionary support Jeffrey Van Harte ’80 launched Titan Capital Management which provides students with hands-on investing experience.
