Summer Session: One Step Closer to Graduation at CSUF

Summer Session is open to currently admitted CSUF students. Transfer students admitted for Fall, non-admitted students and members of the public can take summer classes through Open University. Summer Session provides a great opportunity to achieve your academic goals and accelerate your path to graduation. Contact your Student Success Team to find out how you may benefit from taking summer classes.

  • Finish the last units you need to graduate by the end of summer.
  • Increase your GPA.
  • Complete prerequsities, GE courses or internships.
  • Take a class you couldn't get into during Fall or Spring.
  • Online, hybrid and in-person courses are offered.
