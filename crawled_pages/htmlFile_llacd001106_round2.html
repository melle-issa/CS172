Diversity and Inclusion Institutes

Throughout the academic year, SDSU will convene a series of Diversity and Inclusion Plan Development Institutes to provide training and guidance. Each Institute will be offered multiple times in each month listed below. For more information about the institutes and support offered, visit the Diversity and Inclusion Planning site. The following contains the tentative timeline and content for the Institutes.

March 2020 Plan Submission opens. Plans will be reviewed and revised with support of Councils
