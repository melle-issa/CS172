Fiduciary Management

  • Fiduciary Management for Trustees
  • Fiduciary Management for Conservators
  • View All Certificate Programs

Fiduciary management programs are created and led by industry leaders currently working in the field. These programs are fully-online and can be completed in 5 to 6 months.

Below you will find a list of available fiduciary management certificate programs.

Professional Fiduciary Management for Trustees

  • Length: 6 months
  • Cost: $2,000
  • Location: Online

Professional Fiduciary Management for Conservators

  • Length: 6 months
  • Cost: $2,000 per certificate
  • Location: Online

* The program information listed above is subject to change.
