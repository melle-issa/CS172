An educated world through diversity driven innovation

UC Riverside provides a rigorous and future-focused environment that prepares graduate students like you to take on the world through transformative, real-world applications. We take pride in cultivating individuals who are personally fulfilled and socially enriched. Our top programs span English to Earth Sciences to Computer Engineering, exposing you to a wide variety of viewpoints, all within a tremendously collaborative community. Our inclusive environment, world-renowned faculty and rigorous courses produce leading professionals who are prepared to tackle the dynamic challenges that face not only California’s fastest growing region, but the world at large.
