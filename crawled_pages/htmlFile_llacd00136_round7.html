Event Strategy and Management

With services ranging from strategy to project management, we support University partners in realizing the vision and goals of Brown events.

A successful event reflects your mission and values among the audiences you want to engage.

Utilizing the latest in event design, strategy, planning and execution, Event Strategy and Management serves as guides and strategic partners to bring quality experiences to a diverse range of audiences. Whether we are managing an event or offering models and strategies for effective event planning, our focus is to help departments and offices at Brown create engaging and meaningful events that reflect the University’s commitment to excellence and align with your priorities.

Who We Are

Event Strategy and Management is a team of seasoned professionals that guides the strategy and project management of high-impact events at Brown.

What We Do

We offer varying scopes of services as strategists, consultants, planners and on-site event managers to help support the goals of Brown events.

Event Policies and Protocols

Understanding the University’s event policies and protocols is an important step toward a successful event.

Plan Your Event

Our Planning Guide and Timeline provides a useful framework for visualizing events and navigating the event planning process at Brown.

Resources

Access a collection of comprehensive guides, training and resources that outline the campus services and support you may explore for your event.
