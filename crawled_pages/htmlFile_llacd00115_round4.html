Alumni Impacting California and Beyond

Across the world Cal Poly alumni are making a difference, giving back and having an impact on their communities. More than ever, we want to share their stories. 

Celebrating the 2022 Honored Alumni Recipients

Friday, Sept. 30, Cal Poly recognize nine distinguished alumni who are making an impact on their industries, inspiring their communities and continuing to honor our Learn by Doing Legacy.

Alumni Communities Give $42,000 to Student Success

Cal Poly Alumni Communities recently came together to donate $42,000 to student success initiatives on campus. The funds were sourced from the net proceeds of events hosted by communities throughout the year and include more than $13,000 earmarked toward scholarships and $8,000 to the Cal Poly Food Pantry.

Announcing the New Latinx Alumni Community

The new Cal Poly Alumni — Latinx Community is an identity-based affinity group connecting alumni to each other and the university — including current students. Complete the group's Interest Form to learn more.
