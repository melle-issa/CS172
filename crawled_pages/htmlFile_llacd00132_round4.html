Online Employment System

Cal Poly utilizes the online employment system, Cal Poly Jobs. All participants involved in the recruitment process, including job seekers, interview committees, employment equity facilitators, department chairs/heads and deans, and administrative support staff are able to perform their functions from any computer with internet access.

Job Seekers are able to:

  • View advertised positions online 24 hours per day/7 days per week (24/7)
  • Apply for any open position 24/7 at Cal Poly Jobs from any computer with internet access
  • Monitor status of your application through individually assigned password protected user account
  • Receive immediate confirmation of electronically submitted application
  • Complete basic application information one time and update/edit, as needed, when applying for other positions.

Human Resources is committed to ensuring that all employees and off-campus applicants have equal access to the employment process. Therefore, a number of cost-free options are available to assist individuals who may have limited computer/internet access or need assistance with the application process.

Upon submission, all application materials become the exclusive property of Cal Poly and will not be copied or returned.
