Welcome to the Sonoma State Police Department

Our team patrols twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week. The police department lobby is open Monday - Friday from 8am to 4:30pm and our staff is ready to help you during business hours. Contact police@sonoma.edu or call (707) 664-4444 to reach our team.

Home

Timely Warnings and Bulletins

Sonoma State Police will log Timely Warnings and Bulletins here.Read more about the criteriafor the issuance of Timely Warnings.

Safety Escorts

Need an escort to your vehicle or dorm room? UPD offers safety escorts 24/7 to our campus community, even during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Livescan Fingerprinting

We offer Livescan fingerprinting services. Please make an appointment, then stop by with a valid picture ID and a Livescan request form.

Submit an Anonymous Tip

Please note that the investigations tip line is monitored during regular business hours. If you are reporting an emergencythat requiresimmediate assistance, please call or text 9-1-1.

Request Report

Have you experienced an emergencyand need a copy of the police report? Complete a report onour new, online form to request a copy of areport.

Text to 911

"Call if you can, text if you can't" is the new slogan for the Text 911 feature recently launched at SSU. Read more about the service and its benefits to the campus community.
