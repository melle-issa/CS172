    Tips to improve your Service Catalog experience

    Requesting Services and Help Online

    You can easily request services and help online, using the Chico State IT Support website.

    Browsing Services

    1. Click on the "Services" tab above, or the large red "Request Service" button below "Live Chat"
    2. Browse through the service categories until you find the service you want
    3. If the service is requestable online, click on the "Request Service" button and fill out the form to request service or help. Your request will be immediately routed to the appropriate technicians.

    Searching for Services

    1. Click on the "Services" tab, above
    2. Click on the "Search" tab in the secondary navigation bar
    3. Enter what you are looking for in the Search field and press Enter
    4. Click on the service you want
    5. If the service is requestable online, click on the "Request Service" button and fill out the form to request service or help. Your request will be immediately routed to the appropriate technicians.

    You can also use the general search bar at the top-right corner of the page, but that will include search results for the knowledge base as well as services.
