Learn state-of-the art manufacturing engineering technologies

The Advanced Manufacturing Engineering Certificate Program offers rich occupational experience.

This certificate program is designed to introduce the principles of advanced manufacturing technologies, while enabling participants to experience rich occupational experience and continued skills improvement. Earning the Advanced Manufacturing Engineering Certificate will enhance the competitiveness, technical knowledge, and resulting working efficiency of the program graduates.

Our Advanced Manufacturing Engineering Certificate program is taught by Instructor Sagil James, Ph.D., Associate Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, California State University, Fullerton.

Meet Dr. Sagil James

Dr. James is currently working as an Associate Professor in Mechanical Engineering at California State University, Fullerton. He received his master’s and Ph.D. degrees in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Cincinnati. His primary research areas are Advanced Manufacturing, Smart Manufacturing, and Process Modeling and Simulation. Dr. James brings in more than 15 years of combined academic research and industry experience in the manufacturing field to his role as the lead instructor for the Advanced Manufacturing Engineering Certificate Program (AMEC) at California State University, Fullerton.

Advanced Quality Engineering

This course introduces the basic concepts of quality engineering. It covers various traditional and advanced quality-related programs that have been widely used throughout the design and manufacturing industries. Specifically, it focuses on statistical process control, six sigma, inspection techniques, sampling, automated inspection, metrology, Coordinate Measuring Machines, Optical, Non-optical inspection techniques, etc.

Product Development Engineering

This course covers various topics in industrial production systems including process planning, integration of product design and manufacturing and other functions through concurrent engineering, Design for Manufacturing (DFM), methods used to implement production planning and control, through material requirements planning, shop floor control, and enterprise resource planning (ERP).

After the completion of this Advanced Manufacturing Engineering Certificate program, the students will obtain the following learning outcomes:
a) A fundamental understanding of various advanced manufacturing processes and the principles that underlie them.
b) An ability to understand and apply various technologies and practices of industrial automation, smart manufacturing, and other emerging manufacturing technologies.
c) An understanding of modeling, analysis, and optimization of various manufacturing processes.
d) An ability to apply knowledge, techniques, skills and modern tools of engineering to solve real-world problems in manufacturing industries.
e) Develop effective skills in professional engineering communication and organization.

1. Junior college, community college, and technical college graduates looking for high-technology/high-paying manufacturing jobs
2. Domestic/international undergraduate students seeking manufacturing career-oriented certificate program
3. Fresh university graduates seeking specific job training and skills for manufacturing career
4. Young professional engineers seeking career-boosting certification and increase the potential for a higher salary
5. Manufacturing industries that wish to provide customized manufacturing training to their employees and associates

The program is open to all junior college students, current undergraduate students, graduate students, and eligible employees identified or recommended by their hosting organization or corporation. In order to ensure that students in a course possess the required knowledge and skills and in order to maintain high-quality courses/programs.

It is recommended that participants who reside outside of United States and whose first language is not English possess English language proficiency equivalent to:
A minimum TOEFL score of 550 (paper) or 80 (iBT) Or A minimum IELTS score of 6.5. There is no need to send the test score to the University. It the participant's responsibility to demonstrate English language proficiency necessary to fully participate in the class lectures and discussions.
