Submit the California Dream Act Application (CDA) or Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) for the 2023-24 Academic year to be considered for financial aid!

For additional support with your CDA/FAFSA application, visit our YouTube Channel.

Message from the Office of Financial Aid:

Dear Titans,

In these unprecedented and challenging times, our strength shines through in how we care for each other as we navigate these uncertain times together.  We are please to announce that the Office of Financial Aid is now serving students in-person and virtually, with full services available to meet all of your financial aid needs.

Please see our new extended in-person and phone hours above for fall 2022.

The Office of Financial Aid thanks you for your understanding and patience during this busy time, and we look forward to hearing from you, Titans. And as always remember, Titans Reach Higher! 

IMPORTANT DATES

  • Financial Aid Verification Document Submission Deadline: Thursday, April 27, 2023

NEWS AND UPDATES:

  • Have you received an email from the California Student Aid Commission (CSAC) stating that you have been awarded the Middle Class Scholarship (MCS)?  Due to updates to the award, our office is working with CSAC to post the awards in January. 

  • The Department of Education announces Verification Requirement Waivers for 2022-23 aid year.  All eligible verification documents have been waived.

Has your Family Income changed?
If your family's income has significantly decreased since completing the 2022-23 CDA/FAFSA you may be eligible for a Projected Year Income Appeal. Please contact us for more info.
