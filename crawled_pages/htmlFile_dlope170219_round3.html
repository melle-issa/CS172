Love Museums? Here’s how to earn a Certificate or Masters Degree in Museum Studies at CSU, Chico

The Museum Studies program at CSU, Chico is one of the premiere hands-on museology programs in the United States. Museum Studies Courses Include:

  • Exhibit Research, Design, and Installation
  • Collections Management
  • Museum Education
  • Museum Administration
