Bio

I started contributing to WordPress since 2015. I have been a part of the community from small themes to core contributions. I am a Product/QA Manager, Research Expert, Contributor, Translator, WordPress Organizer/Speaker.
I was a speaker & organizer in WordPress Udaipur 2017. I am a GTE of the WordPress Hindi Project.

Interests

Traveling, Foodie, Team Communication, Research on new things & most loved one WordPress Contribution

WordPress Origin Story

Before I contributed to WordPress, I just knew its basic. When I first contributed to WordPress and my name came up in the contribution list, I was very happy. I got a chance to connect with many people from WordPress and that is what made me to get involved in WordPress And after getting inspired to the contribution, I have contributed in every possible way and today my WordPress profile is my identity.
