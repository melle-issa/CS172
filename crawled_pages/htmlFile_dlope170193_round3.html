Be a part of the College of Communication & Education

The College of Communication & Education (CME) is committed to preparing students for leadership roles in their communities, professions, and relationships. To meet the needs of a diverse society, we continue to build learning environments that focus on combining disciplinary knowledge and personal development with practical application.

Each of our programs and departments present students with unique opportunities that challenge and enrich their university experience. Some of the opportunities available include quality internships, attentive mentorship, real-world training, and other applications focused on professional preparation.
