The Department of Outreach and Recruitment at California State University, Fullerton is dedicated to serving prospective students and their families in the information seeking and application process to the California State University system, and Cal State Fullerton specifically. This includes providing guidance to first-time freshman and prospective transfer students on admissions requirements, financial aid information, and CSU application workshops.

Our department is also home to Campus Tours where a team of Outreach and Recruitment Counselors, Titan Student Ambassadors, and Transfer Peer Advisors work to continuously inform prospective and incoming students of support services that are beneficial towards their successful transition to college.

Outreach & Recruitment Webinar Series

 The Outreach & Recruitment team is proud to bring prospective freshman and transfer students the information they need to ensure a smooth application cycle to California State University, Fullerton. In this virtual environment, our OR counselors and campus partners will host live webinars where students and parents can ask questions and get the answers they seek. Each presentation will be recorded, and following its completion we will link the recording from YouTube. We hope to "see" you there!
