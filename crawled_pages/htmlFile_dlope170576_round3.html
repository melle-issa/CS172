Who we are

The WordPress open source, free software project is the community behind the application you know and love. Powered by volunteers around the world, these groups represent the local gatherings of WordPress enthusiasts. From developers to designers, entrepreneurs to publishers, these events are for people who love WordPress and want to share their knowledge/experience and meet other WordPress users in the area. This WordPress Meetup is open to all who love WordPress --- join us! https://make.wordpress.org/community/meetups
