Stanford Guest House

Book Now

 

Nestled in the Palo Alto foothills on the grounds of the SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory, the Stanford Guest House is a convenient, comfortable and peaceful place to stay. The only hotel owned and operated by Stanford University, it features friendly staff, below-markets rates and exceptional amenities. The guest house is within close proximity to the Stanford campus, Stanford Health and Stanford Children’s Health, Palo Alto Caltrain and destination dining and shopping areas.

We welcome guests of Stanford University, SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory and Stanford Medicine. Guests require both an affiliation to one of these organizations and an educational purpose for their visit. Please call (650) 926-2800 for qualifications and reservations.
