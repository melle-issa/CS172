Media Arts, Design, and Technology Department

Welcome to the home of the Media Arts, Design, and Technology Department where we celebrate the synthesis of art, craft, and technology. Please find information on our programs: the BFA in Communication and the BA in Media Arts with Options in Production and Criticism on the left side of the page.

MADT also offers minors in Social Media Production and Analysis(opens in new window), Broadcasting, Communication Design, and a certificate in Web Design and Publishing.

Students, you can find faculty and advisor office hours (where you can drop in for assistance) on the left menue under Contact Us.  If you have any other questions, please contact the Department Chair Thomas Welsh.

BFA in Communication Design
Communication design is the process of communicating with meaningful visual form integral in graphic design, web design and product design careers.
Media Arts Production Option
Media Arts with an option in Production teaches skills necessary in television, film and new media industries both behind the scenes and in front of the camera.
Media Arts Criticism Option
Media Arts with an option in Criticism teaches skills necessary to critically examine the concepts of visual message design and create solutions to real world projects.
METX

A collaboration between MADT, JOUR and CAGD where students create commercial-quality media for local Chico companies. Want to learn more?

Be a Social Media Boss!

Do you want to learn how to use social media like a pro? Sign up for the minor in Social Media Production and Analysis. Check out the catalog for more details. Be sure to consult your advisor and submit a program plan change to officially add the minor.

KCSC Radio is Now a Part of MADT!

KCSC Radio has made the move from Associated Students in the BMU to the Media Arts, Design, and Technology Department in Tehama Hall 308! KCSC Radio is run by student interns, so if you are interested in being live and on air, please contact Prof. Jennifer Meadows to find out how you can be a part of the station!
