Office of Substance Use Programs Education & Resources (SUPER)

Main content start

SUPER provides research-based alcohol and drug prevention and early intervention programs, education and resources for the Stanford community via peer educators and professional staff. Our goal is to mitigate high-risk alcohol and other drug usage and their related harms among Stanford students by utilizing health promotion principles through collaborative, cutting-edge, empirically-proven educational strategies and programs. Learn more about SUPER.

5-SURE Safe Rides

5-SURE is for all Stanford students, faculty and staff. Drivers are trained to handle a variety of situations. The service assists thousands every quarter providing transportation across campus locations that range from parties to dormitories and libraries. 

Learn More

Party Planning Guide

The party planning guide is an educational social host resource that provides the Stanford student community with up-to-date information, guidelines, policies and laws to help ensure party success, safety and fun for all attendees.

Learn More

Cardinal Recovery supports students in recovery

From “I love someone in recovery” pins to flyers about substance-free socials, members of peer group Cardinal Recovery are making a name for themselves on campus.
