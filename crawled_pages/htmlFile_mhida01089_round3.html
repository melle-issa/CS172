Center for Teaching and Learning

Student Learning Programs

As part of the Stanford Center for Teaching and Learning, our programs are designed to teach transferable academic skills, create a safe space to take intellectual risks, and help all undergraduate and graduatestudents become self-directed learners. We hope to provide you with the tools necessary to help you achieve your academic goalsno matter where you are in your learning process.
