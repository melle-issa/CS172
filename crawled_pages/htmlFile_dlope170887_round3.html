WordPress Management and Design Credential

The WordPress Management and Design series of courses is the first WordPress Credential education program in the world. Individuals who have completed all three (Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3) courses have mastered front end development. They have a unique understanding of Internet business, data networking and website design.

WordPress Management and Design Credential holders have validated their understanding of important aspects of WordPress and its websites. This credential is designed for anyone who wants to work in the WordPress space or learn to build WordPress websites. Once certified, these individuals are ready to pursue WordPress web design, e-commerce, JavaScript, databases and other fields.

Average Salary- $66,540 per Year

The 2023 annual mean salary of a WordPress front end developer is $66,540 USD. This salary was calculated based on the 14,000+ IT professionals who responded to the 2016 IT Skills and Salary survey that was conducted by Global Knowledge in September of 2015.

Exam Requirements and Recertification

To meet the credentialing requirements CertifyWP advisory board has determined that credential holders must re-take the credentialing exam every 3 years to maintain their credentialing. If there are any new developments or software changes to WordPress, our advisory Board will make the corrections and the exam will reflect the changes.

Along with exam questions a practical exam is required to receive the WordPress Management and Design Credential. This practical exam shows that each credential holder not only has a firm understanding of the needed technical skills but can also execute the skills.

The Exam will be proctored by CertifyWP approved education bodies. This is to ensure everyone receives the same exam experience. CertifyWP maintains an equal opportunity for everyone who wishes to take the exams.
