Prepare to become a California educator with CSUF

Through immersive and personalized instruction, our educator programs are designed to help educators fulfill their professional needs, from completing their credentials to meeting prerequisites. Each program offers multiple opportunities for hands-on learning and application of the skills you learn.

Below you will find a list of available programs for educators.

CSUF Educator Courses and Programs:

Autism Added Authorization

  • Length: 3 months
  • Cost: $4,392* (excludes materials)

Education Specialist Induction Program

  • Length: 1 to 2 years
  • Cost: $4,392 - 5,490*

Early Childhood Special Education (ECSE) Bridge Authorization

  • Length: 8 weeks
  • Cost: $1,098*

Improvement Science for Education Leaders

  • Length: 2 semesters
  • Cost: $366 per unit plus campus fees

Level Up! Game-Based Learning Microcredential

  • Length: 10 weeks
  • Cost: $195* per microcourse

Postsecondary Literacy & Learning Certificate

  • Length: 10-12 months
  • Cost: $4,887*

Prerequisites for Special Education (SPED) Preliminary Credential Program

  • Length: 12 months
  • Cost: $3,294*

Single Subject Credential Prerequisites

  • Length: 8 weeks
  • Cost: $4,392*

Teacher Induction Program

  • Length: 10 - 16 months
  • Cost: $3,250 - $5,850*

Teaching for Equity and Engagement in Mathematics (TEEM)

  • Length: Varies
  • Cost: Varies

Test Preparation

  • Length: 4 days
  • Cost: $370 - $500*

Non-California Residents

Due to state and federal regulations, non-California residents may only enroll in CSUF online courses when their state has authorized CSUF as a provider. You can learn more about online program eligibility on the State Regulations page.
