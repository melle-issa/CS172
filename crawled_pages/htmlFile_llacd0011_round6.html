COVID-19

Current CDC Level: Low

SJSU is prepared to shift our COVID-19 safety protocols based on improved community metrics from the Centers for Disease Control (CDC).

Learn more on how the current level changes mask and testing requirements.

Students

Visit the Medical Services page or email studenthealthcenter@sjsu.edu.

Employees/Volunteers

Visit the University Personnel website or email up-vaccine-mgmt@sjsu.edu. 

Mask Status

Masks are recommended for at-risk individuals in indoor spaces around campus and are required in the Wellness Center.

Vaccination Information

For information on the California State University vaccination policy and how to verify your vaccination status, as well as the university mask policy, visit the Vaccination page.

COVID-19 Dashboard

For worksites affected and the number of cases at SJSU, visit the COVID-19 dashboard page.

Report a Case

If you have tested positive for COVID-19, complete the confidential Report a Case form.

COVID-19 Safety Supplies

Use the COVID-19 Safety Supply Request Form to request masks and other personal protective equipment (PPE) and sanitary supplies.

Adapt Plan

See how SJSU handled the COVID-19 and repopulation of campus.
