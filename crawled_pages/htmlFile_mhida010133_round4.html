Winter Session

Earn Credit in 3 or 5-Week Courses

Winter Session: One Step Closer to Graduation

California State University, Fullerton’s Winter Session is open to CSUF students and the public. It is a great opportunity to take required courses and get closer to graduation. Classes are offered on-campus, online and around the world.

Make the most of Winter Session at CSUF

Registration opens in October

Winter Session 2023 Dates

Winter Session runs from December 17, 2022 – January 20, 2023. Classes are offered in two sessions and are 3 or 5 weeks long. For other important Winter dates and deadlines, visit the Dates page.
