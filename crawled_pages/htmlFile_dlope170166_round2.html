How it works

What Is A Social Media Aggregator?

It pulls selected posts across multiple social media accounts to display them in a single, customisable and embeddable-anywhere feed.

Three Step Installation
Yep, it’s Easy.

  • Connect your social media accounts
  • Customise your feed
  • Copy and paste the embed code

Analytics and Reporting
