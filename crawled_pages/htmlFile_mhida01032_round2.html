    About our Plant Sale Fundraisers

    Our community support group — the Friends of the UC Davis Arboretum and Public Garden — holds plant sale fundraisers offering an incredible selection of attractive, low-water, easy-care plants perfect for our region. These sales are held several times a year as large-scale, festival-style events at the Arboretum Teaching Nursery. Watch for the latest information here and in our digital newsletter. 

    Most of these plants we offer at these sales are grown on site, including the Arboretum All-Stars — our top recommended plants for Central Valley gardens. Our nursery staff, in addition to dozens of students and volunteers, propagate and grow the majority of plants we sell.

    By shopping these sales, you support the UC Davis Arboretum and Public Garden's environmental leadership programs, the growth of our gardens and collections and the wide variety of free, educational events we offer the public.

    FAQs

    Have questions? Browse the FAQs below or contact us at arboretum@ucdavis.edu.

    • Why aren't you hosting online plant sales?
    • We know our community was clamoring for us to return to in-person sales. We heard you! In addition, we don't currently have the technology, or the resources, to manage both in-person and online sales simultaneously.
    • Are you going to resume online plant sales in the future?
    • We know the online sales were popular with many customers and are considering ways to bring online sales back in the future on a more limited basis.
