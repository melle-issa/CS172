Join Us at the College of Business

Chico State’s College of Business is one of just 15 percent of all business schools in the world that meet the rigorous standards for accreditation by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business International (AACSB International).

We are a community that cares – instructors, alumni, recruiters all agree that Chico State’s College of Business is Company Worth Keeping. We can’t wait for you to join us!

Refer a Friend

Do you want to win $10,000? Refer a first-time freshman or transfer student to enroll in the College of Business and be entered into a drawing for fall 2023! 

In order to be eligible for the drawing, the referred student must be enrolled as a new student in Fall 2023 and a declared business major. The drawing will take place in September 2023. Refer a friend to the College of Business today!
