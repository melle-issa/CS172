  • Beyond Brilliant
 
As America’s fastest rising university, UC Riverside is acclaimed for creating an academic environment and peer network that supports students like no other comparable campus in the nation. Beyond Brilliant, UC Riverside’s first fundraising initiative dedicated to student-focused priorities across the university, will create even more opportunities for Highlanders to shine.

Let’s be brilliant together.

With your help, we will raise $50 million over the next 5 years to expand need- and merit-based scholarships and fellowships to broaden access and ensure affordability to our world-class educational experience. And we’ll create additional resources, opportunities, and programs for students to shine brightly. With your philanthropic support of Beyond Brilliant, we can propel students into bright futures.

Impacts

Expand Access and Opportunity

Your support makes a UC Riverside education attainable for California’s best and brightest, helping students overcome obstacles as they seek admission and work toward degree completion. 

  • Increasing undergraduate student scholarships
  • Increasing graduate student fellowships

MAKE A GIFT

Energize Learning and Discovery

Your support empowers innovative teaching and learning strategies and funds the creation of inspiring spaces such as state-of-the-art labs and classrooms equipped with experiential technologies.

  • Expanding undergraduate and graduate student internships and experiential learning
  • Inspiring spaces and capital projects to enhance the student experience

MAKE A GIFT

Enhance Health, Well-being, and Sense of Belonging

Your support funds the resources, centers, and programs that provide for students’ basic needs, nurture mental and physical wellness, and foster our culturally diverse campus community.

  • Caring for students’ basic needs and improving health and well-being 
  • Enriching student groups and programs

MAKE A GIFT

Match Update

The Trustee Student Support Match Challenge created a tremendous outpouring of support for UCR students since the launch of Beyond Brilliant on October 4, 2021. This match is now closed and we look forward to launching another match opportunity in the future. Thank you to all of our donors who participated to increase the impact for Highlanders!

Generosity inspires generosity

If you are interested in inspiring the next wave of philanthropic support for UCR students, please consider making a gift to bolster future matching efforts. Contact Nesha Crossman, Senior Executive Director of Individual Giving, at nesha.crossman@ucr.edu or (951) 235-6571 to learn more and discuss options.
