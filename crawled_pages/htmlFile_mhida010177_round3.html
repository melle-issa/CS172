Stanford Web Services

We provide the Stanford community a full range of website planning, design, development, and maintenance support at below-market rates.

 
 

SWS professional service offerings include helping set up your website on the Stanford Sites Drupal Platform.

To learn more, see our detailed guide to planning a new website or jump right into building with this quickstart guide to Stanford Sites.

Website Building

We offer an array of visually compelling solutions with flexible page layouts, dynamic content types, and easy-to-use tools for managing your content in a Stanford-branded design.

Learn more about Website Building

Website Consulting

Leverage our web design and development experience at Stanford to guide you through the complexities of your projects. We can help you craft a comprehensive web project strategy, engage services of highly-qualified vendors, or advise you how to improve the experience of your website visitors.

Learn more about Website Consulting
