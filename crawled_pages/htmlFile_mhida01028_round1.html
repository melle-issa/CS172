Annual Security and Fire Safety Report

The Jeanne CLERY Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act of 1998, commonly referred to as the "CLERY Act," requires institutions of higher education receiving federal financial aid to report specified crime statistics on college campuses and to provide other safety and crime information to members of the campus community. This report is available on the UC Riverside Chief Compliance website.
