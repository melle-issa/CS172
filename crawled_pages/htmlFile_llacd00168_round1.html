COVID-19 and public health updates

Latest Updates

On April 19, 2023, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention updated COVID-19 vaccine recommendations. The Moderna and Pfizer monovalent two dose primary series is no longer recommended for use in the United States and those who have never been vaccinated are now recommended to receive one bivalent dose instead of the discontinued two dose monovalent series. The CDC also recommends an additional bivalent dose for those 65 years and older and for those who are immunocompromised. For those students and employees who are currently compliant with the UC Vaccine Policy, no additional action is required as a result of these updated CDC recommendations. If you have questions about the updated recommendations please call or email Contact Tracing and Vaccine Navigation Services at 949-824-2300 or contacttracing@uci.edu.

Antigen Test Kits and Face Coverings

Antigen test kits and face coverings are available to all students, faculty and staff.
