Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:
A. Relate LSS concepts to business objectives
B. Identify waste within the value stream
C. Demonstrate ability to effectively analyze and present data
D. Define and apply team leadership tools
E. Collect and process customer input/requirement
F. Identify key metrics for measuring success
G. Define the DMAIC process
H. Effectively use tools and concepts associated with each phase of the DMAIC process
I. Employ Lean Six Sigma skills in process improvement projects
