Student Resources

Transfer Student Success

SDSU recognizes and values the wealth of experiences our transfer students bring to our community. You can find resources and opportunities to engage earlier in your academic and professional career, and connect with other transfer students through the official transfer student organization, the Transfer Student Outreach Alliance.

  • Transfer Student Success
  • Transfer Student Outreach Alliance

Online Resources

Use the following websites to determine how transfer course credits can be used to meet SDSU General Education and major preparation course requirements. The Transfer Admission Planner (TAP), updated annually, includes course equivalency and grade requirement information for colleges and universities throughout the US. The ASSIST website is the official repository of articulation for California's public colleges and universities.
