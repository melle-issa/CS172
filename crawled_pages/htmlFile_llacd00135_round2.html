By Students, For Students

Associated Students, a student-run organization, plans events and programming like GreenFest; manages the Aztec Recreation Center and the Conrad Prebys Aztec Student Union, among other facilities; and works to ensure that your Aztec experience will be unforgettable.

Since its inception, Aztecs Rock Hunger has raised millions pounds of food for the Jacobs and Cushman San Diego Food Bank to fight food insecurity in our community.

Our Values

We honor the value and dignity of all individuals by nurturing a university environment that affirms and leverages a diversity of identities, traditions, heritages, perspectives and experiences.

Community of Care

Need support or just have questions? You are never alone – holistic programs created for SDSU students ensure your university experience is healthy and successful.

Health & Wellness

SDSU’s community of care focuses on supporting people and providing resources dedicated to your complete well-being.

Counseling, Psychological Support

We offer counseling, group therapy, time with therapy dogs Baxter and Luna, workshops and more to help you address your concerns and personal growth goals.

Services for Students

If you are looking for answers to your financial questions, searching for an internship or job, want to learn about cultural centers and activities or connect with students who share your interests and passions, the Division of Student Affairs and Campus Diversity is here for you.
