Master's in Technology Leadership

Equipping innovative thinkers with the strategic leadership skills to manage technological change in today's global economy.

Access Application Request Information

This rapidly evolving climate requires a new type of leader, one who can think strategically, with an ability to guide people through transformational change. To thrive, you need to complement your technical expertise with solid leadership, communication and relational skills.

Brown’s Master of Science (ScM) in Technology Leadership goes beyond a traditional MBA — it is a transformative 16-month program designed for busy, ambitious professionals who seek to, or are poised to take on a larger leadership role within their organization. Our rigorous curriculum, taught by world-class faculty, will teach you how to break down industry silos, build cross-function collaborations and leverage a diversity of perspectives. We develop technology leaders by equipping them with a broad vision and ability to innovate in a complex business climate.

Structured for professionals, our program blends online learning with in-person sessions so you can earn a Brown degree and build the professional network and leadership skills vital for advancing your business — without interrupting your career
