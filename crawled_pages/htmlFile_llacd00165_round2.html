The Weber Honors College Mission

The Weber Honors College is about much more than aspiration and academic excellence. It is also about providing a unique community and a gathering space for a diverse group of students who share a commitment to achievement, to giving back to the communities in which they live and work, to deep engagement, and to interdisciplinary conversation.
