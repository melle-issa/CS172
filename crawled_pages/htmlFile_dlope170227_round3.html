WELCOME TO THE 2022-2023 SEASON OF YOUR NORTH STATE SYMPHONY!

Our new season is called Experience Joy. Last season we returned to live, in-person music last year after a year away from the hall. We look forward to sharing the full potential of NSS in all its glory with surprises and goodies for everyone. We'll see you at the symphony!

North State Symphony is a vibrant professional symphony orchestra performing in Chico and Redding, California, as well as Red Bluff, Oroville, and other cities in the North State. We get support from generous individuals and businesses in our communities, as well as Chico State University. Join us for a concert or one of our fun events and celebrate live music in the North State!
