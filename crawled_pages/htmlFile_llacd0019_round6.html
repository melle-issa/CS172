Professional Education is a more flexible way to go to college and advance your career. Courses are available on campus, off-campus, online and during academic gap periods. All courses are taught by SJSU faculty for college credit. Courses are available through the following programs:

Academic Programs: Earn a graduate degree or certificate to advance your professional career while continuing to work.

Open University: Disqualified SJSU students and the general community interested in taking college courses may enroll.

Winter Session: Earn up to 4 units in 2 weeks on campus or online to lighten your spring course load.

Summer Session: Take 5 to 10-week courses on campus or online to get ahead or catch up on classes you need.

CEU Professional Development: Partner with SJSU to help students from a variety of professions meet their industry's continuing education requirements.
