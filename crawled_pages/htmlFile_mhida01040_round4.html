College of Business and Economics Student Success Initiatives

The Student Success Team within the College of Business and Economics is here to support you. Connect with representatives from the offices listed below to identify services and opportunities that will help you achieve your goals.

  • Business Advising: Connect with an advisor by appointment, live chat, or email and find answers to FAQ.
  • Career Services: Schedule a career advising appointment (zoom or in-person), register for workshops and career fairs, and access career resources.
  • Business Tutoring: Schedule an appointment (zoom or in-person).
  • Graduate Programs: Graduate students can schedule appointments, email an advisor about their master's program or submit graduate forms.
  • College Academic Units: Obtain permits or connect with faculty members through the college's six academic units.

Make All the Right Connections

You will establish a strong professional network with more than 9,000 business students, more than 60,000 alumni worldwide and considerable interaction with area business leaders through internships, workshops and mentorships.

An Immersive Experience

Your involvement in clubs, student leadership, volunteer work, international study trips and dozens of other opportunities provide a well-rounded, rich college experience and résumé-building skills.

Fertile Ground for Jobs

Southern California is the right place to grow your career with the region’s strong and diverse business economy providing exceptional professional opportunities.

A Degree and Timeframe
that Works for You

By taking advantage of our extensive support programs, you will receive the assistance you need to graduate on time with the experience and knowledge to launch your career.
