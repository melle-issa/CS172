Brown University’s 255th Commencement

May 26-28, 2023

Commencement is an important milestone for all Brown students — the culmination of their educational journey as undergraduates, graduate or medical students. It’s a time of celebration for many in our community, including graduates, families, friends and alumni. If you are planning to join us in 2023, please consider making arrangements early to ensure the best possible experience.
