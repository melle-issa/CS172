National Geographic Adventurer of the Year chronicles groundbreaking ascent to the top

Come join in for an evening of inspiration and humor as National Geographic Adventurer of the Year 2019, Maureen Beck, shares her amazing journey as a leader in the sport of paraclimbing.

Maureen's one-handed climbing style, developed through trial and error, has earned her two world championships and taken her to some of the most challenging climbs in the world. Don't miss this opportunity to hear about her latest quest to conquer Canada's Cirque of the Unclimbables and attempt the first unassisted adaptive climb of the Lotus Flower Tower.

Maureen's trademark wit and humor will keep you on the edge of your seat as she shares her passion for overcoming obstacles and reaching new heights.
