The One Aggie Network brings together alumni, faculty, friends, parents and families, retirees, staff and students to better serve the needs of its constituents and the university. Members of the One Aggie Network enjoy trips, wine tastings, unique networking opportunities, VIP access for campus events like Picnic Day, university updates and news, and other opportunities that inform, engage and inspire alumni to support UC Davis.
