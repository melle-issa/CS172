Student Portals

(these require student's SDSUID to log in)

  • AidLink - AidLink is a secure online system that gives students 24-hour access to their SDSU financial aid records and SDSU scholarship application(s). Where students activate and manage documents for grants, financial aid and loans.
  • Canvas - Canvas serves as the primary learning management system for SDSU. Faculty can post course content, recorded lectures, YouTube videos, discussions, grades, tests and more. See IT Services for more information.
  • Get Card (SDSUCard) - manage your money, order food, do laundry & more!
  • Handshake - allows students to more easily connect with employers and search for jobs, internships, event information, and on-campus recruiting opportunities
  • IT Services - your student can use their SDSUID to securely access many campus IT services and is their single identifier to access all IT services, workstations/devices, and labs at SDSU. Password management is self-service and recoverable through a variety of options, all selected and managed by the student.
  • Parking - students and guests can purchase permits, pay citations, and appeal citations. Please make sure you have your vehicle information prior to purchasing a permit as it will be required the first time you register in the portal.
  • Web Portal - Admission status check, class schedule and registration, transcripts, official contact information.

Where to suggest your student turn first, if the issue is:

  • A roommate ...
    Resident Assistant (RA) or Residence Hall Coordinator in the hall
  • Personal ...
    Counseling & Psychological Services
  • A cold or flu ...
    Student Health Services
  • Selecting a major ...
    Office of Advising and Evaluations or Career Services
  • Changing a major ...
    Office of the Registrar or academic department
  • Study skills ...
    Counseling & Psychological Services or Academic Advising Center
  • Trouble with a class ...
    First, course instructor; then, department office
  • Trouble with a course instructor ...
    Department chair or Office of the Student Ombudsman
  • Food/meal plans ...
    Aztec Shops student meal plan
    Email: [email protected]
  • Changing an address ...
    Student address: Student’s Web Portal
    Account parent address: email [email protected] or call 619-594-1653
  • Applying for scholarships ...
    Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships
  • Finding employment ...
    Career Services
  • Residency classifications ...
    Office of the Registrar
  • Involvement opportunities, joining a club or organization ...
    Student Life & Leadership
