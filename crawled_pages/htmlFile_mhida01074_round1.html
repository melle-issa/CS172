How to Apply

UCR strives to accommodate applicants with disabilities. In the event that your disability prevents you from completing the online application process, please contact us so that we can provide options to assist you in completing the application process. Please contact disabilitymanagement@ucr.edu or (951) 827-2561 for assistance.
