    The Titan One-Stop Shop (TOSS) Will Help You Figure It Out

    The Titan One-Stop Shop provides many important services that will support you throughout your journey at CSUF. The TOSS is a centralized space that connects students with a group of friendly and supportive TOSS Information Specialists. Get help regarding: Admissions, Financial Aid, Registration & Records, and Student Business Services (SBS). If we are unable to help, we will connect you with someone who can further assist you.
