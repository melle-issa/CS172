Bio

Meher is the COO (Chief Operating Officer) at KDC and also a Codeable Expert.

She is a front-end developer, specializing in WordPress. She takes care of building and maintaining premium websites for her clients and delivers them within tight deadlines. A strong communicator, and highly motivated, Meher is equipped with a sharp understanding of business.

She has worked with multiple brands, and agencies to build their dream website for over 15+ years.

Along with a creative flair for design and aesthetics, she also has an analytical bent of mind that makes her a distinguished talent from the crowd.

Apart from her remarkable talent for understanding clients and delivering the best to her clients, she is also highly motivated, organized, and proactive.
