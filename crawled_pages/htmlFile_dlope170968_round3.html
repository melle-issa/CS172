Welcome to WordCamp Jinja 2023!

Welcome, one and all, to the wondrous world of WordCamp Jinja 2023, where creativity meets technology and the magic of WordPress comes alive!

Are you ready to join us for a WordPress adventure in the heart of Uganda?

WordCamp Jinja 2023 is happening on Monday 4th – Tuesday 5th September 2023 at Iganga Secondary School. And that’s not all! We have an extra Contributor Day on Wednesday 6th September 2023 at The Innovation Village in Jinja City!

Our theme for this year is: ‘There is always more to learn!‘ We believe that learning is a lifelong journey, and WordPress is a great companion along the way. Whether you are a student, a teacher, a developer, a designer, a blogger, or an entrepreneur, there is always something new to discover and share with WordPress. That’s why we have prepared some exciting and engaging activities for you: –

Workshops: Learn from the experts through the Learn WordPress platform, a free online resource for anyone who wants to learn WordPress. You will get hands-on and minds-on experience with creating and accessing courses, lesson plans, quizzes, workshops, and more! And accomplish planned hands-on code labs in the school computer lab to connect theory and practice!

Entrepreneurship Track: Hear from inspiring speakers who have used WordPress to build successful businesses and careers. You will learn tips and tricks on how to start, grow, and scale your online presence with WordPress.

Contributor Day: Join the global WordPress community and make a difference by contributing to the WordPress project. You can help with coding, testing, translating, documenting, designing, marketing, and more! –

Website Hackathon: Put your skills to the test and create a stunning website with WordPress in a limited time. You will work in teams and compete for prizes and glory!

WordCamp Jinja 2023 is not only for the students and teachers. We also welcome the local tech communities – including the Google Developer Group of Jinja and Women Tech Makers – to get involved with WordPress and join the fun!

Don’t miss this opportunity to learn, connect, and celebrate WordPress!
