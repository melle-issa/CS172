Welcome Sonoma State University Alumni!

Representing more than 74,000 graduates, the SSU Alumni Association awards student scholarships, hosts annual programs and scholarship fundraisers, supports campus and community events, and keeps graduates up to date on University news and events.

The mission of the SSU Alumni Association is to foster, renew and enhance the relationship between alumni and the University. The Association is committed to providing service to Sonoma State University alumni as well as providing opportunities for alumni to volunteer, participate and support the advancement of SSU.

 

Thank you to our 22/23 corporate and community partners!
